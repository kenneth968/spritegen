# Character Atlas Layout Guide

Use this when the output combines one full-body character and eight emotion heads.

- The canvas is 1024x1024.
- The left 512x1024 region is one full-body character.
- The right 512x1024 region is a clean 2 by 4 grid of eight 256x256 chibi heads.
- Every head must be the same character identity, costume, palette, and rendering style.
- Keep seams straight and clean at x=512 and between every 256x256 head cell.
- Do not let heads overlap neighboring cells or drift into the full-body region.
